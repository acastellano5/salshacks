FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where programmers and people interested in computer science get together to collaborate, compete, and learn something new! There'll be workshops and speakers. Everyone will create their own project in teams of up to 4 (or you can work individually), and will present their projects at the end. The best projects will be awarded prizes, and there'll be swag and food for everyone! Hackathons are the perfect opportunity for anyone with an interest in computer science or tech to connect with others and develop their skills.

What if I don't know how to code?

No problem! Our hackathon is open to all skill-sets. If you're interested in tech or computer science but don't know where to start, this is the perfect event for you! Instead of coding a project you could create a presentation about your idea. Even if you don't think you're that interested, why not give it a shot? Additionally, we'll be holding beginner workshops to introduce you to programming!

Who can particpate?

All middle and high school students are welcome!
